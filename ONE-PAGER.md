                                    StableBase: Setting a New Standard for Stablecoin Stability

# Overview

StableBase is an innovative stablecoin protocol designed to maintain price stability in the cryptocurrency market. Leveraging the Collateralized Debt Position (CDP) mechanism, StableBase introduces two innovative policy tools: **user-set Reserve Ratio** and **pre-paid Shielding Rate**, to autonomously regulate the money supply and generate yield for stablecoin holders, all without relying on traditional interest rates.

# Key Innovations

1. User-Set Reserve Ratio

   - Empowers advanced borrowers to define a portion of borrowed stablecoin as mandatory reserves to be locked into Reserve Pool.
   - Enables advanced users of the system to borrow at 0% interest rates.
   - Allows dynamic adjustment of the money supply based on market conditions by varying the reserve ratio.
   - Empowers advanced users to act as rate governors, responsible to set the target shielding rate of the system, without any additional governance tokens.
   - Offers amplified yield (2:1) on locked reserve pool deposits compared to stability pool deposits..

2. Pre-Paid Shielding Rate
   - Introduces a prepaid model where users pay upfront for redemption protection over a set period.
   - Rates are determined by Rate Governors, who are responsible for maintaining system stability.
   - Shifts away from the traditional postpaid interest rate model, offering a novel way to borrow and generate yield.
   - Provides a low-risk borrowing option, with users only exposed to standard liquidation risks while benefiting from redemption protection.
   - Fee paid is distributed to the Reserve Pool and Stability Pool depositors.

# Why StableBase?

1. **Autonomous Stability**: The system adjusts the money supply independently, maintaining price parity with the pegged currency.
2. **User Empowerment**: Users manage key parameters without needing complex governance mechanisms.
3. **Yield Generation**: Provides opportunities for yield, benefiting stablecoin holders and rate governors.
4. **Alternative Model**: Bridges the gap between protocols offering 0% interest rates and those with positive interest rates, offering a balanced approach that could set a new industry standard.

# Industry Context

- The stablecoin industry has evolved significantly since the launch of MakerDAO's DAI, with several protocols like Curve USD and Liquity USD becoming key players in the space.
- Liquity v1's no-interest model faced challenges due to a weaker incentive structure. The upcoming Liquity v2 aims to improve on this with user-defined interest rates.
- StableBase sets itself apart with its unique prepaid model, offering a compelling alternative to both traditional and emerging stablecoin protocols.

# Potential Impact

StableBase stands out as a robust alternative to existing stablecoin protocols. By offering a middle ground between 0% and positive interest rates, StableBase has the potential to redefine industry standards by delivering both stability and predictability, while adapting to diverse market conditions.
